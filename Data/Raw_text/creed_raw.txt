

The House of Creed is an authentic, luxury perfume house dedicated to the creation of highly original, artisan fragrances for men and women, made from the finest ingredients the world has to offer.

A unique story. The House of Creed is one of the world’s oldest yet most modern fragrance houses, spanning seven generations from father to son for over 250 years. As the only fragrance house to rely on an ancient infusion technique to capture the raw and rare materials of each perfume, Creed has created a legacy of unrivaled scents, both commissioned and worn by kings, queens and discerning fragrance connoisseurs throughout history.

Our heritage. Based in Paris, with a factory in Fontainebleau, Creed manufactures many of its own essences using a traditional infusion technique which enables Creed to maintain the superior quality and authenticity of its fragrances.
